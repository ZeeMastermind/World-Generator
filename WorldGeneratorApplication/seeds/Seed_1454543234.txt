-----Cuzufoju world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life, and almost everyone uses it.
The source of magic is blood.
Around magic, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the distant future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 20 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has high amount of life, with few barren areas.
The world is 15% water.
