-----cusesukepu world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is virtually nonexistent.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
The source of magic is monstrous bloodlines. However, some mages use inner power/strength.
Around magic, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the distant future of Earth.
Technology is powered by magic.
Around technology, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 0 degrees Celsius. It is extremely cold.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 52% water.
Large glaciers cover much of the world, growing each winter.
