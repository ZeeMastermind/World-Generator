-----Fuxovuka world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by many.
     In some places, Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is pacts with monsters and demons.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the near-future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is the distant future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 24 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 89% water.
The ocean is boiling hot.
