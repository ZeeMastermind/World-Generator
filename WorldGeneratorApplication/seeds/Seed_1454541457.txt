-----Aleke world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life.
     In some places, Every sentient being can use magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by many.
The source of magic is souls. However, some mages use nature.
Around magic, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of bows, plows, and maybe copper.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with worship.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 5 degrees Celsius. It is extremely cold.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 25% water.
Large glaciers cover much of the world, growing each winter.
