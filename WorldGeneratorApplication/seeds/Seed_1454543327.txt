-----Tuciboco world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
     In some places, Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life, and almost everyone uses it.
     In some places, Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is monstrous bloodlines. However, some mages use alchemy.
Around magic, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of aqueducts and better ships than in the past.
     In some places, Technology is prehistoric, consisting of bows, plows, and maybe copper.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 13 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has high amount of life, with few barren areas.
The world is 87% water.
