-----Muju world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is virtually nonexistent.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
The source of magic is memorized words of power and spells.
Around magic, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 1 degrees Celsius. It is extremely cold.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 21% water.
Large glaciers cover much of the world, growing each winter.
