-----Cicexanu world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by many, but not all.
     In some places, Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life, and almost everyone uses it.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is souls.
Around magic, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
     In some places, Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of aqueducts and better ships than in the past.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations. However, some have built technology from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 7 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has high amount of life, with few barren areas.
The world is 99% water.
The vast majority of the world is frozen over.
