-----Yalesi world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is nature.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
Technology is similar to how it is on earth. However, some have built technology powered by magic.
Around technology, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 10 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 59% water.
