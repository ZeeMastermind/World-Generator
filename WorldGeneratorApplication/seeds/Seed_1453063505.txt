-----ujupa world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Every sentient being can use magic.
The source of magic is monstrous bloodlines. However, some mages use pacts with monsters and demons.
Around magic, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of bows, plows, and maybe copper.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
     In some places, Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 19 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 67% water.
