-----Kadokufiku world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is memorized words of power and spells.
Around magic, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 13 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has an extremely high amount of life, and all ground is covered with vegetation.
The world is 64% water.
