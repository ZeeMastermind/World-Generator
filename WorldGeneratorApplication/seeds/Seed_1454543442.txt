-----Vamane world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
     In some places, Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is deities or spirits.
Around magic, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
     In some places, Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
Technology is powered by magic.
Around technology, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 28 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 78% water.
Rain evaporates immediately upon hitting the ground.
