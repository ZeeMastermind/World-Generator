-----depipuza world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is monstrous bloodlines.
Around magic, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the distant future of Earth.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 4 degrees Celsius. It is extremely cold.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 81% water.
