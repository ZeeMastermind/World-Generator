-----wafemere world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by many.
The source of magic is inner power/strength.
Around magic, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 3 degrees Celsius. It is extremely cold.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 72% water.
