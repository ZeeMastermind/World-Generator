-----Nocacu world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is deities or spirits. However, some mages use nature.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the distant future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is the near-future of Earth.
Technology is powered by magic. However, some have built technology based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 2 degrees Celsius. It is extremely cold.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 33% water.
Large glaciers cover much of the world, growing each winter.
