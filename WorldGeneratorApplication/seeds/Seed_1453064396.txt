-----siviwori world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
     In some places, Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is memorized words of power and spells.
Around magic, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
     In some places, Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
Technology is powered by magic.
Around technology, people react with worship.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 1 degrees Celsius. It is extremely cold.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 26% water.
