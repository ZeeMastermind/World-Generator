-----qugekolasu world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is virtually nonexistent.
     In some places, Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is monstrous bloodlines.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of bows, plows, and maybe copper.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
     In some places, Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations. However, some have built technology based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with worship.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 29 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 82% water.
