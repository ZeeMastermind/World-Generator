-----Azayejaga world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
     In some places, Magic is used by many.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
The source of magic is nature. However, some mages use deities or spirits.
Around magic, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of bows, plows, and maybe copper.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic. However, some have built technology based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with worship.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 20 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 0% water.
