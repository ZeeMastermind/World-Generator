-----Baloheti world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by many.
     In some places, Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is alchemy.
Around magic, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
Technology is powered by magic. However, some have built technology similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 13 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has an extremely high amount of life, and all ground is covered with vegetation.
The world is 42% water.
