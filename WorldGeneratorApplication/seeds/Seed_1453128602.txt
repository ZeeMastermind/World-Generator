-----Quyi world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is alchemy.
Around magic, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with worship.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 24 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 40% water.
The ocean is boiling hot.
