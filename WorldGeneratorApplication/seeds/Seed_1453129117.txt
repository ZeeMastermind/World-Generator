-----Bosuxaqolo world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life.
     In some places, Every sentient being can use magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by many.
The source of magic is deities or spirits. However, some mages use nature.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
     In some places, Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 8 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has high amount of life, with few barren areas.
The world is 15% water.
