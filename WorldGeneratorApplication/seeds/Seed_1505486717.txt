-----Wuhowe world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is souls. However, some mages use monstrous bloodlines.
Around magic, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
     In some places, Technology is prehistoric, consisting of bows, plows, and maybe copper.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 17 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has an extremely high amount of life, and all ground is covered with vegetation.
The world is 48% water.
