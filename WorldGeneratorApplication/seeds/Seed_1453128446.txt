-----Rulisologu world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by many.
The source of magic is pacts with monsters and demons. However, some mages use alchemy.
Around magic, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the near-future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is the distant future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations. However, some have built technology similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 11 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 26% water.
