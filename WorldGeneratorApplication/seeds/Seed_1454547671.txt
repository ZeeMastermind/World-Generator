-----Iti world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is alchemy.
Around magic, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth. However, some have built technology similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 21 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 78% water.
Rain evaporates immediately upon hitting the ground.
