-----Gukakije world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Every sentient being can use magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by many.
The source of magic is monstrous bloodlines.
Around magic, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 13 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 91% water.
