-----Jiwiyefi world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Every sentient being can use magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by many, but not all.
The source of magic is nature.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the near-future of Earth.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 23 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has high amount of life, with few barren areas.
The world is 77% water.
Rain evaporates immediately upon hitting the ground.
