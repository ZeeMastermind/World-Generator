-----Zije world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is inner power/strength.
Around magic, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
Technology is powered by magic. However, some have built technology similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 22 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 91% water.
The ocean is boiling hot.
