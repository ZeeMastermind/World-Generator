-----Ivedu world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is virtually nonexistent.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
The source of magic is alchemy.
Around magic, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of aqueducts and better ships than in the past.
Technology is powered by magic. However, some have built technology from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 14 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has an extremely high amount of life, and all ground is covered with vegetation.
The world is 89% water.
