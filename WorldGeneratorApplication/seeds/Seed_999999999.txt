-----Nuxugaci world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by very few.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
     In some places, Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is memorized words of power and spells.
Around magic, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
Technology is similar to how it is on earth. However, some have built technology based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 5 degrees Celsius. It is extremely cold.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 36% water.
Large glaciers cover much of the world, growing each winter.
