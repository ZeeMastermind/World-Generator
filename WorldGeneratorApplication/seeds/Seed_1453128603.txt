-----Jasa world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life.
The source of magic is pacts with monsters and demons.
Around magic, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic. However, some have built technology from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 8 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 83% water.
