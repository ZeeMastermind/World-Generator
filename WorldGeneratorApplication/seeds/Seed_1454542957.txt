-----Biyuxize world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by very few.
     In some places, Magic is used by many.
     In some places, Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is monstrous bloodlines.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
Technology is powered by magic.
Around technology, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 19 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 50% water.
