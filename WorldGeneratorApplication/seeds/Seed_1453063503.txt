-----xoviku world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is nature.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
Technology is powered by magic.
Around technology, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 22 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 76% water.
The ocean is boiling hot.
