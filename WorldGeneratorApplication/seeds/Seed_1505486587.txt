-----Molakayipe world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by very few.
     In some places, Magic is used by many, but not all.
     In some places, Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is alchemy. However, some mages use inner power/strength.
Around magic, people react with worship.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
     In some places, Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
Technology is powered by magic. However, some have built technology steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 24 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 25% water.
The ocean is boiling hot.
