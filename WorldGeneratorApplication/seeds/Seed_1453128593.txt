-----Domedise world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is nature. However, some mages use souls.
Around magic, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
     In some places, Technology is prehistoric, consisting of bows, plows, and maybe copper.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 4 degrees Celsius. It is extremely cold.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 83% water.
The entire world is frozen over.
