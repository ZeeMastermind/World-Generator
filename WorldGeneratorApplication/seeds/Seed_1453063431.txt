-----sotukijuwo world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by many.
The source of magic is monstrous bloodlines.
Around magic, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the distant future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is the near-future of Earth.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology. However, some have built technology steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 12 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 35% water.
