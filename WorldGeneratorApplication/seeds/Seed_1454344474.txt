-----Soretogala world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Every sentient being can use magic.
     In some places, Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life.
The source of magic is inner power/strength.
Around magic, people react with worship.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
     In some places, Technology is prehistoric, consisting of bows, plows, and maybe copper.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 10 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 65% water.
