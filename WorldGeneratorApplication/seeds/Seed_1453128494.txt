-----Kelexa world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by many.
     In some places, Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is pacts with monsters and demons. However, some mages use pacts with monsters and demons.
Around magic, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic. However, some have built technology from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 0 degrees Celsius. It is extremely cold.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 67% water.
