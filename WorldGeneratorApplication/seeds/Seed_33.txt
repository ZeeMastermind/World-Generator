-----rujeyu world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Every sentient being can use magic.
     In some places, Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life.
The source of magic is alchemy.
Around magic, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the near-future of Earth.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 20 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 86% water.
