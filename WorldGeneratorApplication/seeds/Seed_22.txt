-----fogejoli world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life.
     In some places, Every sentient being can use magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by many.
The source of magic is monstrous bloodlines.
Around magic, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the near-future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 1 degrees Celsius. It is extremely cold.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 65% water.
