-----Vipude world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by very few.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
     In some places, Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is inner power/strength. However, some mages use alchemy.
Around magic, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 26 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 15% water.
Rain evaporates immediately upon hitting the ground.
