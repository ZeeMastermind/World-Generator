-----Foganuxexu world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Every sentient being can use magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by many.
The source of magic is memorized words of power and spells.
Around magic, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the near-future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology. However, some have built technology similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 10 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 20% water.
