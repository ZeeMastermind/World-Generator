-----Teye world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
     In some places, Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life, and almost everyone uses it.
     In some places, Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is memorized words of power and spells.
Around magic, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with hostility.

--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with hostility.

----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 12 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 17% water.
