-----Wadowodule world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Every sentient being can use magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by many.
The source of magic is blood.
Around magic, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations. However, some have built technology based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with worship.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 16 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 15% water.
