-----Hejemetu world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
     In some places, Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life.
     In some places, Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is pacts with monsters and demons.
Around magic, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
Technology is powered by magic.
Around technology, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 11 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 84% water.
