-----figuco world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by many.
The source of magic is deities or spirits.
Around magic, people react with worship.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 12 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 86% water.
