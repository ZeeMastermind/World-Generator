-----Xiji world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
     In some places, Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life.
     In some places, Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is memorized words of power and spells.
Around magic, people react with worship.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 21 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 24% water.
The ocean is boiling hot.
