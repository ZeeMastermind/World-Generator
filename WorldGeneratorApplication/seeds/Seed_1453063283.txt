-----aqujifewo world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is virtually nonexistent.
     In some places, Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is inner power/strength.
Around magic, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of bows, plows, and maybe copper.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
     In some places, Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
Technology is powered by magic. However, some have built technology similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 9 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 28% water.
