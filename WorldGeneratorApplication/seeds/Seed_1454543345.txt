-----Jejo world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by many, but not all.
The source of magic is memorized words of power and spells.
Around magic, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 5 degrees Celsius. It is extremely cold.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 77% water.
Large glaciers cover much of the world, growing each winter.
