-----Emohigi world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is deities or spirits.
Around magic, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
     In some places, Technology is prehistoric, consisting of bows, plows, and maybe copper.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 11 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 66% water.
