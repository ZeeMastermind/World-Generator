-----Eya world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is virtually nonexistent.
     In some places, Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is monstrous bloodlines.
Around magic, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
     In some places, Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 14 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 53% water.
