-----Kinoqu world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by many.
     In some places, Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is alchemy.
Around magic, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the near-future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology. However, some have built technology powered by magic.
Around technology, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 26 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 70% water.
The ocean is boiling hot.
