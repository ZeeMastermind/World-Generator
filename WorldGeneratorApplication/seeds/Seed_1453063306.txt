-----dekico world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is souls. However, some mages use souls.
Around magic, people react with worship.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations. However, some have built technology based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 16 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 69% water.
