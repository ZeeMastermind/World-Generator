-----Lokuwaxase world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by very few.
     In some places, Magic is used by many.
     In some places, Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is souls. However, some mages use pacts with monsters and demons.
Around magic, people react with admiration.

--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the near-future of Earth.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 8 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 79% water.
