-----Yegejayahe world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is nature.
Around magic, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of aqueducts and better ships than in the past.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic. However, some have built technology based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 8 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 71% water.
