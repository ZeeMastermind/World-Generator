-----peduke world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is monstrous bloodlines.
Around magic, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 4 degrees Celsius. It is extremely cold.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 35% water.
The entire world is frozen over.
