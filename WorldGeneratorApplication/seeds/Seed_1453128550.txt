-----Dibeweve world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is deities or spirits.
Around magic, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the distant future of Earth.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 13 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 94% water.
