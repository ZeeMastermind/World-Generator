-----uqo world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is alchemy.
Around magic, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 6 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 28% water.
