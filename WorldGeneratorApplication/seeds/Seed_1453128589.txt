-----Alohiru world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is inner power/strength. However, some mages use nature.
Around magic, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 15 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 61% water.
