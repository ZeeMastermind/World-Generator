-----tota world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is souls. However, some mages use deities or spirits.
Around magic, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
Technology is similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 8 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 86% water.
