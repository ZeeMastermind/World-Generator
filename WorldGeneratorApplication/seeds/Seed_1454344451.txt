-----Gihaha world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Every sentient being can use magic.
The source of magic is memorized words of power and spells. However, some mages use blood.
Around magic, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of bows, plows, and maybe copper.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth. However, some have built technology based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 24 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 68% water.
The ocean is boiling hot.
