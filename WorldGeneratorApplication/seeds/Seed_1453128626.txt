-----Tihito world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by very few.
     In some places, Magic is used by many.
     In some places, Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is blood. However, some mages use memorized words of power and spells.
Around magic, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
Technology is powered by magic.
Around technology, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 8 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 59% water.
