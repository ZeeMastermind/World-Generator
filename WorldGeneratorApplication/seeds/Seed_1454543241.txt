-----Dokozojano world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is virtually nonexistent.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
The source of magic is nature. However, some mages use inner power/strength.
Around magic, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 9 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 92% water.
