-----Elu world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is virtually nonexistent.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
The source of magic is nature.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 4 degrees Celsius. It is extremely cold.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 83% water.
