-----Ikepano world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is monstrous bloodlines.
Around magic, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of bows, plows, and maybe copper.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic. However, some have built technology powered by magic.
Around technology, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 19 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 70% water.
