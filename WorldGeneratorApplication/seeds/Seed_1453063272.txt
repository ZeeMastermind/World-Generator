-----javofonobi world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life.
The source of magic is memorized words of power and spells.
Around magic, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
     In some places, Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 20 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 31% water.
