-----Jisota world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Every sentient being can use magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by many.
The source of magic is pacts with monsters and demons.
Around magic, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the near-future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is the distant future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth. However, some have built technology based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 13 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has an extremely high amount of life, and all ground is covered with vegetation.
The world is 70% water.
