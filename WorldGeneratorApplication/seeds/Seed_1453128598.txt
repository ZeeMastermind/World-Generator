-----Mijegaxe world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
     In some places, Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life.
     In some places, Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is deities or spirits. However, some mages use alchemy.
Around magic, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
     In some places, Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 9 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 30% water.
