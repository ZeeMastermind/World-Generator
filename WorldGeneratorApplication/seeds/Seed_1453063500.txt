-----jenugu world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
     In some places, Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is monstrous bloodlines.
Around magic, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is ancient, consisting of aqueducts and better ships than in the past.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 19 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 1% water.
