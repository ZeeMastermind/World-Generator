-----Riwebariza world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by many.
     In some places, Every sentient being can use magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
The source of magic is pacts with monsters and demons. However, some mages use pacts with monsters and demons.
Around magic, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 20 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has high amount of life, with few barren areas.
The world is 71% water.
