-----Sino world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
     In some places, Magic is used by many, but not all.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
The source of magic is deities or spirits.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
     In some places, Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations. However, some have built technology steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 26 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 28% water.
