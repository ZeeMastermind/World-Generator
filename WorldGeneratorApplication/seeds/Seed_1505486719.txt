-----Lobeqa world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life, and almost everyone uses it.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is blood.
Around magic, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth. However, some have built technology from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 12 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 64% water.
