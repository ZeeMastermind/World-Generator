-----Surirefajo world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Every sentient being can use magic.
The source of magic is nature.
Around magic, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of aqueducts and better ships than in the past.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 5 degrees Celsius. It is extremely cold.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 78% water.
