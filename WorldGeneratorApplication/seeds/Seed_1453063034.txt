-----gutiji world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
     In some places, Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is alchemy.
Around magic, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 5 degrees Celsius. It is extremely cold.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 60% water.
