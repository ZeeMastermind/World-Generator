-----Wikekeyixi world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by very few.
     In some places, Magic is used by many, but not all.
     In some places, Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is pacts with monsters and demons. However, some mages use alchemy.
Around magic, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the near-future of Earth.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 28 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 68% water.
