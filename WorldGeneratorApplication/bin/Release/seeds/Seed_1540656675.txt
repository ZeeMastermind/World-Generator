-----Norabu world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
     In some places, Magic is used by many, but not all.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
The source of magic is nature.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the near-future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is the distant future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic. However, some have built technology steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 12 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has high amount of life, with few barren areas.
The world is 41% water.
