-----Lesexi world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
     In some places, Magic is used by many, but not all.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
The source of magic is deities or spirits.
Around magic, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
     In some places, Technology is the near-future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 6 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 92% water.
