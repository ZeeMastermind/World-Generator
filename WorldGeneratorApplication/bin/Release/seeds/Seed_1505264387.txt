-----Iwu world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by very few.
     In some places, Magic is used by many, but not all.
     In some places, Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is monstrous bloodlines.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the near-future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is the distant future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 19 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 38% water.
