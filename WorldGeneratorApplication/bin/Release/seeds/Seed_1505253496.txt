-----Muqi world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
     In some places, Magic is used by many, but not all.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
The source of magic is memorized words of power and spells.
Around magic, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
     In some places, Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 0 degrees Celsius. It is extremely cold.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 99% water.
Large glaciers cover much of the world, growing each winter.
