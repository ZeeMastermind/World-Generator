-----Haqehupesa world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by very few.
     In some places, Magic is used by many, but not all.
     In some places, Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is deities or spirits. However, some mages use souls.
Around magic, people react with worship.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
     In some places, Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of aqueducts and better ships than in the past.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 14 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 59% water.
