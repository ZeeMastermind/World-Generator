-----Cavevezoqu world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
     In some places, Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life, and almost everyone uses it.
     In some places, Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is souls. However, some mages use inner power/strength.
Around magic, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is ancient, consisting of aqueducts and better ships than in the past.
Technology is powered by magic.
Around technology, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 22 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has high amount of life, with few barren areas.
The world is 38% water.
