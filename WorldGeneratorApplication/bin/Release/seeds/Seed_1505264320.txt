-----Zigexufa world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Every sentient being can use magic.
The source of magic is blood. However, some mages use monstrous bloodlines.
Around magic, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
Technology is powered by magic. However, some have built technology similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 12 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 71% water.
