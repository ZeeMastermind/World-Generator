-----Amama world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is virtually nonexistent.
     In some places, Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is alchemy.
Around magic, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the near-future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is the distant future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 9 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 8% water.
