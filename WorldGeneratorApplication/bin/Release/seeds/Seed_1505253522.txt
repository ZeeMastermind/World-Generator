-----Razanu world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
     In some places, Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is inner power/strength.
Around magic, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of aqueducts and better ships than in the past.
     In some places, Technology is prehistoric, consisting of bows, plows, and maybe copper.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 27 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 42% water.
