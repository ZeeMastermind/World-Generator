-----Equze world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by many, but not all.
     In some places, Every sentient being can use magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
The source of magic is alchemy. However, some mages use blood.
Around magic, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
     In some places, Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
Technology is similar to how it is on earth. However, some have built technology powered by magic.
Around technology, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 21 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 50% water.
