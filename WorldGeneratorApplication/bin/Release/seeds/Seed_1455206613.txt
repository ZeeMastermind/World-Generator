-----Cipiqiwari world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is virtually nonexistent.
     In some places, Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is pacts with monsters and demons.
Around magic, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
     In some places, Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 25 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 63% water.
Rain evaporates immediately upon hitting the ground.
