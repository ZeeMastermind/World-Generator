-----Fovu world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is pacts with monsters and demons.
Around magic, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
     In some places, Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
Technology is powered by magic. However, some have built technology powered by magic.
Around technology, people react with worship.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 9 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 99% water.
