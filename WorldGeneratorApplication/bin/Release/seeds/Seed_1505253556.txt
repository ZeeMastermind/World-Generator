-----Batime world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Every sentient being can use magic.
     In some places, Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life, and almost everyone uses it.
The source of magic is deities or spirits.
Around magic, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of aqueducts and better ships than in the past.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 9 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 73% water.
