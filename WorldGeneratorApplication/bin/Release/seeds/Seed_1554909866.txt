-----Duye world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is virtually nonexistent.
     In some places, Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is souls.
Around magic, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
     In some places, Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic. However, some have built technology powered by magic.
Around technology, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 10 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has high amount of life, with few barren areas.
The world is 26% water.
