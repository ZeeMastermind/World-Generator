-----Wili world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is inner power/strength. However, some mages use memorized words of power and spells.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is ancient, consisting of aqueducts and better ships than in the past.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 28 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 0% water.
Rain evaporates immediately upon hitting the ground.
