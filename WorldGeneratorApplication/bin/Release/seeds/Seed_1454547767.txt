-----Renujatedo world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is inner power/strength. However, some mages use memorized words of power and spells.
Around magic, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the distant future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations. However, some have built technology steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 12 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 2% water.
