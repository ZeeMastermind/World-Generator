-----Cujowa world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is pacts with monsters and demons.
Around magic, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
     In some places, Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 6 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has high amount of life, with few barren areas.
The world is 73% water.
Large glaciers cover much of the world, growing each winter.
