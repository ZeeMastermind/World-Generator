-----Haliqaso world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Every sentient being can use magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by many, but not all.
The source of magic is alchemy. However, some mages use deities or spirits.
Around magic, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of bows, plows, and maybe copper.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
     In some places, Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 19 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has high amount of life, with few barren areas.
The world is 59% water.
