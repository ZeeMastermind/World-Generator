-----Biqacivo world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is alchemy. However, some mages use blood.
Around magic, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of bows, plows, and maybe copper.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
     In some places, Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 20 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 85% water.
