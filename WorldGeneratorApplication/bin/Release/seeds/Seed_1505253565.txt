-----Bucehoyi world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
     In some places, Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life, and almost everyone uses it.
     In some places, Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is memorized words of power and spells.
Around magic, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
     In some places, Technology is the near-future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
Technology is powered by magic.
Around technology, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 2 degrees Celsius. It is extremely cold.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 57% water.
