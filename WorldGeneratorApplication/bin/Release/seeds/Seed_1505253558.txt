-----Iza world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is virtually nonexistent.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
The source of magic is inner power/strength.
Around magic, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
     In some places, Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 21 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 80% water.
The ocean is boiling hot.
