-----Noyodine world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
     In some places, Magic is used by many, but not all.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
The source of magic is memorized words of power and spells.
Around magic, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 20 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 41% water.
