-----Kewiyewa world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Every sentient being can use magic.
The source of magic is inner power/strength.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of bows, plows, and maybe copper.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth. However, some have built technology similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 19 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 34% water.
