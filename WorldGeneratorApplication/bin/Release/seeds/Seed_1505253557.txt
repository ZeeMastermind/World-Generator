-----Sota world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Every sentient being can use magic.
The source of magic is souls.
Around magic, people react with admiration.

--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with hostility.
     In some places, people react with indifference.

----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 15 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 71% water.
