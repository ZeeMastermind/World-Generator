-----Rumozajiyi world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is blood. However, some mages use blood.
Around magic, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 12 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 21% water.
