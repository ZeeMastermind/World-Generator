-----Fonulufi world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by very few.
     In some places, Magic is used by many, but not all.
     In some places, Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is memorized words of power and spells. However, some mages use pacts with monsters and demons.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of rocks and sticks.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology. However, some have built technology steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 8 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 97% water.
