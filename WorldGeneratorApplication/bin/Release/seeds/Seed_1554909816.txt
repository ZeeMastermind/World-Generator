-----Gononawe world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by many, but not all.
The source of magic is blood.
Around magic, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the distant future of Earth.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth. However, some have built technology similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 26 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 63% water.
