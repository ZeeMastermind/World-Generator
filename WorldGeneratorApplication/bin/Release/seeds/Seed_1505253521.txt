-----Asacoze world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by many, but not all.
     In some places, Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life, and almost everyone uses it.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is blood. However, some mages use pacts with monsters and demons.
Around magic, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth. However, some have built technology similar to how it is on earth.
Around technology, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 21 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 24% water.
