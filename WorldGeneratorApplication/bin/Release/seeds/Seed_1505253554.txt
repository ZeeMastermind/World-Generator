-----Hiseyabe world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by many, but not all.
     In some places, Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life, and almost everyone uses it.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is pacts with monsters and demons.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the distant future of Earth.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology. However, some have built technology based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 24 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 41% water.
