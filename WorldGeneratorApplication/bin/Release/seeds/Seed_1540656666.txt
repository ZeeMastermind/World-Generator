-----Yedobeqa world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by many, but not all.
The source of magic is inner power/strength.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the distant future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is the near-future of Earth.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth. However, some have built technology steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic.
Around technology, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 23 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 0% water.
