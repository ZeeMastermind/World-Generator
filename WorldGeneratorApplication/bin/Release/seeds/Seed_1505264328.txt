-----Nutavesi world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life, and almost everyone uses it.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is memorized words of power and spells.
Around magic, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 23 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 30% water.
Rain evaporates immediately upon hitting the ground.
