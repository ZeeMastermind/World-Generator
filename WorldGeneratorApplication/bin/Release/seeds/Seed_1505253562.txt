-----Isasoqo world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is memorized words of power and spells.
Around magic, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is ancient, consisting of ironworking and writing.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology. However, some have built technology based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with worship.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 16 degrees Celsius. It is hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 71% water.
