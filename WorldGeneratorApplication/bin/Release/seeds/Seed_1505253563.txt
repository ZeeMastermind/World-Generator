-----Duzagakiki world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by many, but not all.
     In some places, Every sentient being can use magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by very few.
The source of magic is pacts with monsters and demons.
Around magic, people react with discomfort.
     In some places, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with fear.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the near-future of Earth.
Technology is from the ruins of ancient civilizations. However, some have built technology from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 7 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 38% water.
Large glaciers cover much of the world, growing each winter.
