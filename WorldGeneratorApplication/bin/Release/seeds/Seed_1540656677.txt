-----Bima world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
     In some places, Magic is taken for granted as an aspect of everyday life, and almost everyone uses it.
     In some places, Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
The source of magic is pacts with monsters and demons.
Around magic, people react with awe.

--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is the near-future of Earth.
     In some places, Technology is industrial, consisting of the steam engine and electricity.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with hostility.

----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 10 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has an amount of life similar to Earth.
The world is 69% water.
