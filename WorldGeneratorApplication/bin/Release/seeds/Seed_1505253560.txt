-----Docihile world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Every sentient being can use magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by many, but not all.
The source of magic is nature.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with indifference.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the medieval period, consisting of windmills and the dry compass
Technology is similar to how it is on earth. However, some have built technology powered by magic.
Around technology, people react with indifference.
     In some places, people react with awe.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 10 degrees Celsius. It is cold.
The world has an extremely high amount of life, and all ground is covered with vegetation.
The world is 81% water.
