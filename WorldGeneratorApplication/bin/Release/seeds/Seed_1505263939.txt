-----Gidihahofo world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is virtually nonexistent.
The source of magic is inner power/strength.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
Technology is steampunk/cyberpunk in nature, defying standard notions of logic. However, some have built technology from the ruins of ancient civilizations.
Around technology, people react with admiration.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 24 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 42% water.
The ocean is boiling hot.
