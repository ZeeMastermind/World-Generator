-----Tahoyocomo world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Only a few living people have ever seen magic.
     In some places, Magic is used by some, and people know of it.
The source of magic is memorized words of power and spells.
Around magic, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is modern, consisting of nukes and computers.
     In some places, Technology is similar to the renaissance, consisting of gunpowder and the printing press.
Technology is based upon discovered alien technology.
Around technology, people react with awe.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 24 degrees Celsius. It is extremely hot.
The world has virtually no life, and few things survive.
The world is 78% water.
