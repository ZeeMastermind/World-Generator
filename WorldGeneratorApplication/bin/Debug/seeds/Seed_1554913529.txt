-----Cicuwexe world-----

--------------------------------------------
-----MAGIC-----
--------------------------------------------
Magic is used by very few.
The source of magic is monstrous bloodlines.
Around magic, people react with fear.
     In some places, people react with hostility.
--------------------------------------------
--TECHNOLOGY --
--------------------------------------------
Technology is prehistoric, consisting of bows, plows, and maybe copper.
     In some places, Technology is ancient, consisting of aqueducts and better ships than in the past.
Technology is similar to how it is on earth. However, some have built technology powered by magic.
Around technology, people react with admiration.
     In some places, people react with worship.
     In some places, people react with discomfort.
--------------------------------------------
----CLIMATE----
--------------------------------------------
The average temperature of the world is 14 degrees Celsius. It is like earth.
The world has low amount of life, which is hard to find.
The world is 81% water.
